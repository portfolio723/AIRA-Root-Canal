# **App Name**: Aira Dental Clinic: Painless Smiles

## Core Features:

- Hero Section Display: Showcase the primary headline, subheadline, compelling one-image card, and the main 'Book Your Free Consultation' CTA.
- Informative Content Sections: Render dedicated sections for 'Why Root Canal Treatment?', 'Who Needs a Root Canal?', 'Our Treatment Process', 'Why Choose Aira Dental Clinic', and 'Why Early Treatment Matters' with their respective mini trust lines.
- Interactive FAQ Section: Provide an expandable and collapsible section for frequently asked questions and their answers.
- Patient Testimonials Display: A dedicated section to showcase authentic patient reviews to build trust and credibility.
- Call-to-Action (CTA) Integration: Implement clickable buttons for 'Book Your Free Consultation', 'Call Now', and 'WhatsApp' across the page, featuring the '30% off' offer.
- Clinic Location Map: Embed an interactive Google Map displaying Aira Dental Clinic's precise location in Kokapet.
- AI Symptom Assessment Tool: An AI-powered tool that allows users to input general dental symptoms and receive preliminary information or advice, encouraging a professional consultation at the clinic.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional forest green (#066F41) for buttons and key highlights, chosen to evoke trust, health, and a calm, clean clinical environment.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated green-gray (#EAF0EE), subtly hinting at the primary color while maintaining a clean and airy feel for readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear green (#40BF40), providing a complementary contrast for secondary elements and drawing attention where needed without overwhelming the main design.
- Headline font: 'PT Serif' (serif), as requested, for section headers, providing a touch of classic professionalism and authority. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability and modern, clean aesthetic in body text. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize simple, clean line-art icons, especially checkmarks for trust lines, and relevant dental symbols, maintaining a professional and health-oriented aesthetic.
- A modern, responsive, and hierarchical layout inspired by the 'refer image' concept provided (which is not available, so assuming a standard clean design). The design will emphasize clear content sections, ample whitespace, and prominent calls to action, ensuring ease of navigation and a pleasant user experience on all devices.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations such as smooth scroll effects, gentle hover states for buttons, and fluid transitions for the FAQ section's expand/collapse actions to enhance user interaction.
